    I’m a Senior at Duke double-majoring in Computer Science and Biomedical Engineering. Recently at MITRE I built secure internal tools used by thousands of federal agents—owning Flask Blueprint endpoints, Keycloak SSO & group integration, SQLAlchemy/Alembic migrations, and a PostgreSQL backend with a jQuery/AJAX + Foundation 6 front-end. I love hard problems, learning new skills, and turning research ideas into real, reliable software.
